Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

Healthy quinoa bowl with roasted vegetables and tahini dressing.

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • Mixed Mediterranean vegetables
  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package
  2. Roast vegetables with olive oil
  3. Prepare tahini dressing
  4. Assemble bowls
  5. Drizzle with dressing
